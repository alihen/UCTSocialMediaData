University of Cape Town Social Media Data Collection

Data collected from UCT Social media accounts to aid reform in social media & communication policies at the University of Cape Town

View on GitHub Download .zip Download .tar.gz

Why is this being done?

During the Student Fee protests that took place in South Africa from 17-23 October, the University of Cape Town handled communication with students very poorly, not utilising their social media channels or replying to students' queries. The data collected in this project further confirm this and will be presented to the university's communication department with an extensive complaint and the motion to set up social media and communications policies.
